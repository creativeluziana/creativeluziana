## 🌸 About Me

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/creativeluziana/creativeluziana/main/coding.png" align="right" width="320" alt="AboutMe"/>

Hi, I'm **Luziana D'Mello** — a designer at heart and a developer by passion.  
I love crafting digital experiences that not only look great but also feel effortless to use. ✨  

🎨 **Design is my comfort zone** — I enjoy working with color, layout, and interaction to build clean, intuitive interfaces.  

💻 At the same time, I dive into **full-stack development**, bringing those designs to life with **HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind, NextJS** and backend tools like **Node.js and MongoDB**.  

I’m always chasing that **sweet spot between beauty and functionality** — creating things that make life just a little smoother. 🌸
